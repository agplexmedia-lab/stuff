Join AG Plex

Follow these steps to get access. It’s quick, and once you’re in, you’re set.

1

Create a Free Plex Login

Make your account here: https://www.plex.tv/sign-up/

Already have a Plex account? Skip this step.

2

Send Me Your Plex Email

Submit the email you use for Plex using the form below.

Tip: If the form looks weird on your phone, open it in your browser.

3

Accept the AG Library Invite Email

You’ll get an invite email from Plex. Click Accept Invite.

Check spam/promotions if you don’t see it.

4

Download Plex (if needed)

Plex is available on Roku, Apple TV, Firestick, Smart TVs, phones, and computers.

5

Log In & Enjoy

Sign into Plex on your device and you’re good to go. Enjoy All of AG Movies and TV@
