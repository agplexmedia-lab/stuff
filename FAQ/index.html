The Basics AG Plex
Q
What is Plex?
Plex is a popular app amongst media nerds and non-nerds alike. It lets someone (me, Andrew) organize and stream their own collection of movies and TV shows from a central location (my home desktop computer). Anyone I invite can stream my personal collection anywhere in the world for free. You can add Plex on any smart device just like Netflix, Prime Video, etc. Plex also has its own free content, but that's not the fun part and that stuff has ads.
Q
Where do you get your movies and shows?
Everywhere! It's my main hobby to find and collect movies and TV shows. They're stored on my own computer, which streams them back to you on demand.
Q
Is access to AG Media on Plex free?
Yup! I share my libraries (AG Movies and AG TV) completely free. Doesn't cost me anything beyond my own server and Wi-Fi. Make a free Plex account, then request access to my library here :)
Q
I signed into Plex but only see their free junk. Where's the AG stuff?
If you've already followed the steps to get access to my AG Media library and still don't see it, try signing out of the app and back in. That should prompt AG Movies and AG TV to appear on your homepage. On a computer, you may need to click More on the left sidebar.
Q
So you'll just add whatever I want?
Absolutely! I love finding stuff and expanding the collection, especially things you can only find via online rental or on an app you don't have. Just submit a request and I'll find it. Usually within a day!

Using Plex on Your Device How-To
Q
How do I get the AG libraries to show up on my TV (Roku, Apple TV, Android TV, Samsung)?
The first step is always to install the free Plex app from your device's app store and sign into your Plex account. Once you've been given access to AG Plex, the AG Movies and AG TV libraries should show up automatically. If they don't, sign out and back in.

From there, you'll want to pin the AG libraries so they show up every time you open the app without having to dig for them. See below for how to do that.
Q
How do I pin the AG libraries to my sidebar so I don't have to hunt for them?
This works basically the same on Roku, Apple TV, Android TV, and Samsung. Look for More at the bottom of the left sidebar. Scroll through until you find AG Movies and AG TV. There'll be a pin or heart icon next to each one. Select it and they'll show up in your sidebar from then on.

On Roku specifically, once you're in the More section, highlight the library and press the star button on your remote to favorite it.

On Apple TV, go to More, focus on the library, then move left on the trackpad to get the pin option.
Note: if you delete and reinstall the Plex app, you'll need to re-pin everything. The app doesn't sync that stuff to your account.
Q
How do I browse and watch TV shows?
Once AG TV is pinned, select it from your sidebar. You'll land on the Recommended tab which shows recently added stuff and things you've been watching. To see everything, tap the Library tab at the top of the screen. That's the full list of shows.

Pick a show, then pick a season, then pick an episode and hit play. Plex keeps track of where you left off so it'll pick up where you stopped if you come back to something mid-episode or mid-season.
Q
How do I find movies or shows by genre, year, etc.?
Go into the AG Movies or AG TV library and tap the Library tab. Near the top of the screen you'll see filter and sort controls. You can filter by genre (Horror, Comedy, Drama, etc.), sort by release date, rating, or title, or switch between different views. It works on all the TV apps the same way.
Q
What are Collections and how do I find them?
Collections are groups of related movies or shows bundled together, like all the films in a franchise. When you're in the Library tab of AG Movies, look for a Collections tab or filter option near the top of the screen. Selecting it switches you from the full movie list to a view showing all the collections. Tap any collection to see what's in it.

Some collections also appear inline in the main library view mixed in with individual movies. If you see a tile with a stack of posters on it, that's a collection.
Q
How do I get back to something I was watching?
The home screen has a Continue Watching row that shows anything you started but didn't finish. Just select it and it'll jump back to where you were. If you don't see the Continue Watching row on your home screen, make sure the AG libraries are pinned in your sidebar, as that's what makes those rows appear.
