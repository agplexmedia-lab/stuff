Q
What is Plex??
Plex is a popular app amongst media nerds - Plex allows someone (me, Andrew) to organize and stream their own collection of movies, TV shows, music, and photos, from a central location (Andrew’s home desktop computer).
Q
Where do you get your movies and shows?
Everywhere! It is my main hobby to find and collect movies and tv shows.
Q
So you’ll just add whatever I want to this Plex App for me?
Absolutely! I love finding stuff and expanding my collection. Even / especially if it’s stuff you can find only via online rental or on an app you don’t have (apple tv lol). Just submit a request and I will find it! Usually in under a day!
Q
Is Plex Free?
Yup! I have an account, and all these features are free. I also have a Plex Pass for some extra “nerd” settings, but overall, everything is free—apart from my own investments into my own server. If you’d like your own free Plex account, go for it! You'll have your own profile, settings, and history. Then you can request access to my library here :)
