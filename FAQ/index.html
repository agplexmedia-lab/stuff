FAQ

Quick answers for new AG Plex people. If you still have a question, text Andrew.

Q
What is Plex??
Plex is a popular app amongst media nerds — it lets someone (me, Andrew) organize and stream their own collection of movies, TV shows, music, and photos from a central location (Andrew’s home desktop computer).
Q
Where do you get your movies and shows?
Everywhere! It is my main hobby to find and collect movies and TV shows.
Q
So you’ll just add whatever I want to this Plex App for me?
Absolutely. I love finding stuff and expanding my collection — even / especially if it’s something you can only rent online or it’s on an app you don’t have (Apple TV lol). Just submit a request and I will find it — usually in under a day.
Q
Is Plex free?
Yup. Having an account and using Plex is free. I also have a Plex Pass for extra “nerd” settings, but for you, everything is free (aside from my own investments into the server). If you want your own free Plex account, go for it — you’ll have your own profile, settings, and watch history.
Q
How do I request access using my own Plex account?
Use this page: Request access to the library.
Q
Woah, is this legal?
Lol, 100%. All of my content is legally sourced. Plex is legit.
